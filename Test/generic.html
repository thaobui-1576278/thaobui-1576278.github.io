What We Do

We are a brand-new start-up company focusing on integrating the sectors of technology and healthcare.

Our mission is to develop new technologies that will minimize healthcare disparities for vulnerable populations in Washington and educate the public on important healthcare topics.

We currently partner with UW Medicine, Fred Hutch, and Seattle Children's.
