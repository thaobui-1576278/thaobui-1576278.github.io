Tips on Getting Admitted

  • Get good grades & test scores
  • Take challenging classes
  • Answer the essay prompts thoroughly
  • Do running start
  • Be (decently) involved with your community

Tips on Getting Into Your Major & Becoming a Competitive Applicant

  • Stay at Odegaard until 6 AM to study
  • Be naturally smart
  • Ask advice from current/former students in the major
  • Spend time on your personal statement
  • Get good grades (AKA earn above a 3.6+ in every class)
